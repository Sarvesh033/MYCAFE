Our Story

Founded in 2015, The Daily Grind began with a simple mission: to serve the best cup of coffee in the city. We source our beans ethically from sustainable farms across the globe and roast them in small batches right here in our shop.


Whether you need a quiet corner to work, a place to catch up with friends, or just a quick caffeine fix, our doors are always open.
